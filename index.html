About the Station

Debre Birhan FM is a community radio station serving the city of Debre Birhan and the wider North Shewa Zone of the Amhara Region, Ethiopia. The station broadcasts news, music, cultural programs, education, and public-interest content to its local audience and to Ethiopians around the world via live internet streaming.

Programs celebrating the heritage and traditions of the Amhara region.
